# Future AI Fact Checker

## Status

Deferred. The initial Lawyer may suggest evidence to verify, but it must not claim independent research or verification.

## Intended responsibility

A future Fact Checker could evaluate supplied sources or web-retrieved material, distinguish claims from evidence, report verification limits, and attach attributable citations. It must not silently rewrite the public transcript or decide debate outcomes.

Before it is built, decide the web/search provider, source-quality standard, citation storage, privacy policy, error handling, and whether fact-checking occurs during or after a debate.
